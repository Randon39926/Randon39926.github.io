An Introduction to Myself

As far as my primary education entails, I have a strong background working with SQL and a passion for working with Tableau. Since the late spring of 2023 I have worked extensively and only with Tableau and Tableau's incredible ability to graphically represent datasets and charted information to various future clients who would truly appreciate its potential.
