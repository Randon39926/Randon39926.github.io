(Last Updated: 12/6/2023) (Publisher/Site Owner and Creator: Randon Carr)

I would like to introduce myself a bit further to the reader, since you have taken the time to view my website at all.


I spent the majority of my life in Bradenton Florida with my family. I enjoy passing the time by riding motorcycles or learning a bit more about Tableau and various computer based topics. I currently reside in Attica Michigan with my family and my girlfriend where we live the quiet life. I would also like to include that I have also resided in interesting places such as Stafford and Fredericksburg Virginia. Travelling has also been a large part of my life as I have made many road trips back and forth from Florida to Michigan primarily along I-75.

Working with others and becoming a productive individual or team is one of my passions that I enjoy sharing with others. My belief is that hard work and compassion defines who you are. Being approachable is also important! It is difficult to work with others or to be a member of a team if you are not kind and approachable. I thought it would be best to share my beliefs here today to give you a better idea of who I am, and who I can be for you and your company.
